Cluster & Cloud Computing

Australian City Analytics

The project is to develop a Cloud-based solution that exploits a multitude of virtual machines across the NeCTAR Research Cloud for harvesting as many tweets as possible from across the cities of Australia on the NeCTAR Research Cloud. These tweets are stored in CouchDB database. Then they are used for undertaking a variety of social media data analytics scenarios that tell interesting stories of life in your cities. Our team analyzed up to 10 scenarios and importantly inclueded the comparisions with the data available within the AURIN platform to improve our knowledge of life in the cities of Australia. with containing the amalgamated collection of Tweets from the harvester applications.
